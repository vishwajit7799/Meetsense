Frequently asked questions

Does the bot appear in the meeting?
Yes — the bot joins as a visible participant named "MeetSense Notetaker". All attendees can see it. We believe in transparent, consent-aware recording.
What meeting platforms are supported?
Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and Zoom. The meeting link is detected automatically from your calendar event.
How does billing work?
₹100 is charged monthly via Razorpay. You can pay via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit card, credit card or netbanking. Auto-renews each month until cancelled.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period.
Is my meeting data private?
Yes. Transcripts and summaries are stored only in your own database (Neon PostgreSQL). MeetSense is open source — you can verify exactly what happens to your data.
