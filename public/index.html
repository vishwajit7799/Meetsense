How it works

Set up once, never think about it again

1

Connect your calendar

Sign in with your Microsoft 365 account. MeetSense reads your Outlook calendar — nothing else.

2

Bot joins automatically

Two minutes before each meeting, a bot joins via the meeting link. It shows up as "MeetSense Notetaker" in the participant list.

3

Notes land in your inbox

Within 2 minutes of the meeting ending, a structured summary with action items arrives in your email.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

📅

Outlook calendar sync

Connects to Microsoft 365. Detects Teams, Zoom and Google Meet links automatically.

🤖

Headless bot
